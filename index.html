Welcome to Beginner's Universe

Have you ever stared at the stars and wondered what lies beyond our world? This website is for people who are curious about space but don’t know where to start. You don’t need a science background or fancy vocabulary—just a little curiosity and imagination. Beginner’s Universe is a friendly guide to basic astronomy. Here, you can learn about the planets in our solar system, how our galaxy (the Milky Way) works, and fun facts about the universe. We use simple language, clear examples, and helpful images to make space easy to understand. You’ll also find colorful illustrations, basic definitions, and links to explore more. This site is perfect for students, young learners, and anyone who wants to start learning about astronomy in a relaxed and enjoyable way. Whether you want to know how many planets there are, what makes Saturn’s rings special, or where Earth is in the galaxy—you’ll find answers here. Explore the two main parts of this site: The Planets – Learn about the 7 major planets in our solar system The Milky Way – Discover our galaxy and what’s in it Let’s begin your journey through the universe—one fun fact at a time!
